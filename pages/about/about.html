About Sancturia Wildlife

Our Mission

At Sancturia Wildlife, we believe in the power of collective action to make a difference in the world. Our mission is to create a dedicated platform that connects compassionate individuals with wildlife sanctuaries across India, enabling direct support for conservation efforts that protect vulnerable species and their habitats.

Our Vision

We envision a world where wildlife thrives in its natural environment, and communities are engaged in sustainable conservation practices. By fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation for India's rich biodiversity, we aim to inspire a global movement towards wildlife protection and ecological balance.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Sancturia Wildlife emerged from a deep-rooted passion for wildlife conservation and a recognition of the challenges faced by sanctuaries in India. Many sanctuaries operate with limited resources, relying on donations to provide care, rehabilitation, and sanctuary for injured, orphaned, and rescued animals.

How We Work

We provide a user-friendly platform that allows you to explore various wildlife sanctuaries in India and choose one that resonates with you. Through our site, you can make direct donations to support their ongoing conservation projects, ensuring that your contributions make a tangible impact.
